### POC (Proof of Concept)

#### Phase 1: Define Business Problems Suitable for Generative AI
- Identify business problems that can be addressed using generative AI.

#### Phase 2: Assess Data Requirements and Availability
- Evaluate data availability, quality, and accessibility needed for the PoC.

#### Phase 3: Set Clear Success Metrics
- Establish metrics to measure the success of the PoC.

#### Phase 4: Address Responsible AI Considerations and Compliance Risks
- Ensure responsible AI practices and compliance with relevant regulations.

#### Phase 5: Time-box Development Efforts
- Define a clear timeline for development activities.

#### Phase 6: Identify Key Stakeholders and Decision Makers
- Engage key stakeholders and decision-makers in the PoC process.
