#### 1. **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**
   - Calculate comprehensive TCO including model inference costs, infrastructure expenses, development time, and ongoing maintenance.

#### 2. **Cost per Transaction**
   - Establish cost per transaction metrics accounting for all system components.
   - Compare with existing processes to support business case development.

#### 3. **Break-even Analysis**
   - Determine the usage volume required to justify implementation costs.
   - Include both one-time development expenses and ongoing operational costs in calculations.

#### 4. Process Automation Benefits
1. **Time Savings Quantification**
   - Measure time savings achieved through automation.

2. **Quality Improvements**
   - Assess improvements in quality due to automated processes.

3. **Scalability Advantages**
   - Evaluate the scalability benefits of using generative AI solutions.

#### 5. Business Impact Assessment
1. **Revenue Impact**
   - Capture potential revenue opportunities from implementing generative AI.

2. **Risk Reduction**
   - Quantify risk reduction benefits including improved compliance, reduced human error, and enhanced security.

3. **Strategic Value**
   - Consider strategic benefits like competitive advantage, innovation capabilities, and organizational learning.
